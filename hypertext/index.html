The train station

A lonely train station was loud with silence. It was a grand station, paying homage to the artistic prestige of the Gilded Age.
A surplus of stores and eateries littered that station- convenience stores, bagel shops, pizza parlors, newspaper stands, flower shops, and many other odd and ends establishments..but they were all empty
Not a shout of announcement of an incoming train, not a whisper of reprimand from a frazzled mother to her curious child going near the tracks,
not an anxious question from a novice traveler, nor a dissatisfied grunt of a businessman over the delay of a train. All was silent in a still death
A vacant space, a void of eternal quiet,

but wait!!
