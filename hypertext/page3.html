THE REALIZATION

"Where is everyone?" she thought as she looked around and began to walk through the abandoned train station in terror.
She first began to walk, wanting to be reasonable and not jump to the worst conclusions. But the more she explored the station in search of life. The more panicked she became as she realized no one was there.
Running to the exit, she pulled frantically at the doors, but they were stuck and refused to open. Darkness covered the windows, and all the windows showing outside the station revealed a pitch of EMPTINESS..
